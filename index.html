Know Your Setup

Anatomy of a Skateboard

  • 01
    Deck
    The wooden board you stand on. Usually 7–8.5" wide, made from 7-ply maple.
  • 02
    Trucks
    The metal T-shaped axle assembly that connects wheels to the deck.
  • 03
    Wheels
    Polyurethane wheels rated by hardness (durometer). Harder = faster, softer = smoother.
  • 04
    Bearings
    ABEC-rated steel bearings that sit inside each wheel and allow them to spin freely.
  • 05
    Grip Tape
    Sandpaper-like sheet applied to the top of the deck for foot traction.
  • 06
    Hardware
    Nuts and bolts that secure the trucks to the deck. Usually 7/8" or 1" length.
